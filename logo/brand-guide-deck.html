03 · Use it well

Four rules we
won’t bend on.

01

Lead with one color.

Pick cobalt OR ink for the background. Don't mix on the same surface.

02

Lime is a spark.

Use it on accents, buttons, italic emphasis words. Never as a body fill.

03

Italic = Bricolage.

The italic moment in any headline always uses Bricolage's true italic — never simulated.

04

Eyebrows are mono.

JetBrains Mono, uppercase, 0.14em tracking. Always prefixed with //.
